Why do I need to confirm my email address?

We ask that you confirm your email address so that we know we have a way of reaching you for any communications related to the normal operation of One tsp.

These may include

  • Notices about changes to One tsp. and how you access the site
  • Password recovery (if you were to ever lose or forget your password)
  • Payment receipts for Pro Accounts

These WILL NOT include

  • Marketing promotions
  • Spam
